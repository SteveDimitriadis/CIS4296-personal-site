Unit Assessment System

As director of the The Office of Institutional Research and Assessment (IRA), Ms. Gina Calzaferri looks to improve the efficiency of gathering and tracking assessment reports for Temple University. Having worked closely with students to develop the Student Learning Assessment Management System (SLAM), Ms. Calzaferri seeks to assist in the design of an analogous system for administrative and support units. Calzaferri seeks a system that will track and automate the organization and gathering of these assessments. Assessments serve the purpose of complying with accreditation requirements and regulatory standards. Additionally, they give insight to areas of excellence for the University while highlighting areas in need of improvement. To increase efficiency, the Unit Assessment System (UAS) automates gathering and tracking of these reports. UAS will be similar to the S.L.A.M. system in place differing primarily in language and areas of focus.
